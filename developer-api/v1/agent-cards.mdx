---
title: "Agent Cards"
description: "Visa Intelligent Commerce credentials for agent purchases with merchant and amount scope."
icon: "credit-card"
---

When your agent makes a purchase, it generates a unique payment credential through Visa Intelligent Commerce (VIC). Each credential is scoped to the specific merchant and capped at the requested transaction amount.
